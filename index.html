ABOUT ME

I am a game developer and graphic designer based in Poland, passionate about creating immersive spaces where people can explore, connect and have fun.

I am currently a student at Merito University in Poznan majoring in Computer Science with a specialization in Graphics and Multimedia. I graduated from the Technical School with a major in Digital Graphics and Printing, passing the AU.54 and AU.55 professional exams.

As a freelance game developer, graphic designer and participant in various game jams, I continue to develop my skills in these areas. Constantly working on a wide variety of projects is a lot of fun because it gives you the opportunity to interact with new technologies, collaborate with different teams, and continuously grow and learn.

THE TRAVERSE

Traverse is the most dynamic and compelling storytelling experience that can be played in The Sandbox. A story-driven adventure platform game, it follows the journey of plucky explorer Astro whose spaceship crash lands on Mars. There, he finds a sinister society of reptilians led by the evil Melon X. Featuring over 50 NPCs, 30 quests, and three richly layered chapters, players have to use cunning, judgment and agility to guide Astro through Mars, rescue his crew, and return to Earth.

THE CUBE

C.U.B.E, the latest work from GamerHash studio, introduces players to a space training camp unlike anything they've encountered before. Taking place in the vast Sandbox universe, the game puts participants through a series of immersive and varied tasks, from solving puzzles to testing reflexes. As in “The Traverse” project, I worked on the implementation of the game's mechanics, gameplay variety system.

DEADLOCK

Deadlock will take you through enemy lines to defeat their boss and put an end to all the trouble the locals are facing. Show your enemies that there is no one who can stop you from reaching your destination.

The game was created in collaboration with Mikołaj Górowski during 32 bit GameJam. During the short event, we created a short story through which we guide the player in a retro Playstation styled fps game. I worked on player-related mechanics and weapon models.

NO PAIN NO GAIN

No pain no gain is a platform game with the theme “Roles Reversed” developed during GMTK Game Jam 2023 with a group of friends who had no prior experience with the Unity engine. Pain is the only thing that preserves the integrity of the body. Avoid medical kits because they will kill you. Spikes and razor blades are more your idea of fun.

I worked on player-related mechanics, obstacle models and the main menu interface during this project.

Game

DICE MASTER

Dice Master is a “Roll of the Dice” themed puzzle game developed during GMTK Game Jam 2022 and involves positioning yourself on the map to destroy enemy cubes and score points.

It was created in collaboration with Mikołaj Górowski, my role in the project was to create all the logical aspects, moving the dice around the board and destroying enemies.
