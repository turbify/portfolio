ABOUT ME

I am a graphic designer and game developer based in Poland, passionate about creating immersive spaces where people can explore, connect and have fun.

Constantly working on a wide variety of projects is a lot of fun because it gives you the opportunity to interact with new technologies, collaborate with different teams, and continuously grow and learn.

I am currently a student at Merito University in Poznan majoring in Computer Science with a specialization in Graphics and Multimedia. I graduated from the Technical School with a major in Digital Graphics and Printing, passing the AU.54 and AU.55 professional exams.

As a freelance graphic designer, game developer and participant in various game jams, I continue to develop my skills in these areas.

THE TRAVERSE

Traverse is the most dynamic and compelling storytelling experience that can be played in The Sandbox. A story-driven adventure platform game, it follows the journey of plucky explorer Astro whose spaceship crash lands on Mars. There, he finds a sinister society of reptilians led by the evil Melon X. Featuring over 50 NPCs, 30 quests, and three richly layered chapters, players have to use cunning, judgment and agility to guide Astro through Mars, rescue his crew, and return to Earth.
